## Getting Started

### Accessing Lessons

1. **Browse Courses**: Navigate to the Courses page to view all available courses
2. **Enroll in a Course**: Click the "Enroll" button on any course card
3. **Select a Lesson**: Open your enrolled course and click on any lesson to start watching

### Video Player Interface

The video player features:
- **Video Display Area**: Main viewing area for the YouTube video
- **Play/Pause Button**: Control video playback
- **Progress Bar**: Shows current position and allows seeking
- **Volume Control**: Adjust audio level or mute
- **Playback Speed Selector**: Adjust playback speed (0.5x to 2x)
- **Fullscreen Button**: Expand video to fullscreen mode
- **Progress Indicator**: Shows your completion percentage

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Keyboard shortcuts provide quick access to common video controls without using your mouse.

### Essential Shortcuts

| Key | Action | Description |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| **Space** | Play/Pause | Toggle between playing and paused states |
| **←** (Left Arrow) | Rewind 10 seconds | Jump back 10 seconds in the video |
| **→** (Right Arrow) | Forward 10 seconds | Skip ahead 10 seconds in the video |
| **F** | Fullscreen | Toggle fullscreen mode |

### Using Keyboard Shortcuts

1. **Focus the Player**: Click anywhere on the video player or press Tab to focus it
2. **Press the Shortcut**: Use the keyboard shortcuts listed above
3. **Visual Feedback**: The player will respond immediately to your input

**Tips:**
- Make sure the video player is focused (has a blue outline) for shortcuts to work
- Keyboard shortcuts work even when the video is paused
- Use Left/Right arrows to review or skip content quickly

## Playback Controls

### Play/Pause
- **Click** the play button in the center of the video or at the bottom controls
- **Space bar** toggles play/pause when the player is focused
- **Auto-pause**: Video automatically pauses when you navigate away from the page

### Seeking (Jump to Position)
- **Click** anywhere on the progress bar to jump to that position
- **Drag** the progress bar handle to scrub through the video
- **Keyboard**: Use Left/Right arrow keys to jump 10 seconds

### Volume Control
- **Click** the volume icon to mute/unmute
- **Drag** the volume slider to adjust level (0% to 100%)
- **System Volume**: Respects your device's master volume setting

### Fullscreen Mode
- **Click** the fullscreen button (bottom-right corner)
- **Press F key** when player is focused
- **Exit**: Press F again or Esc key to exit fullscreen

## Playback Speed

Adjust playback speed to match your learning style.

### Available Speeds

| Speed | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **0.5x** | Complex topics requiring careful attention |
| **0.75x** | Detailed explanations or note-taking |
| **1x** | Normal playback (default) |
| **1.25x** | Review of familiar material |
| **1.5x** | Quick review or second viewing |
| **2x** | Very familiar content or revision |

### How to Change Speed

1. **Locate** the playback speed selector below the video
2. **Click** on your desired speed (0.5x, 0.75x, 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, or 2x)
3. **Active Speed**: The current speed button is highlighted in blue
4. **Audio Pitch**: Audio pitch is preserved at all speeds for natural listening

**Tips:**
- Start at 1x for new material, increase speed for review
- Slower speeds (0.5x-0.75x) are great for technical demos
- Faster speeds (1.25x-2x) work well for recap videos
- Your speed preference is remembered for the current session

## Progress Tracking

Your video progress is automatically tracked and saved.

### Automatic Saving

- **Save Interval**: Progress is saved every 10 seconds while the video is playing
- **What's Saved**: Your furthest watch position (highest point reached)
- **No Action Needed**: Saving happens automatically in the background
- **Offline Queue**: If you're offline, progress is queued and synced when you reconnect

### Progress Indicator

- **Completion Percentage**: Displayed below the video (e.g., "45% Complete")
- **Progress Bar**: Green fill shows your completion progress
- **Visual Feedback**: "Progress saved" indicator appears after successful saves

### Highest-Position Tracking

The system tracks the **furthest point you've reached** in the video:
- **Forward Progress**: Watching new content increases your progress
- **Backward Seeking**: Rewinding doesn't decrease your saved progress
- **Free Review**: Watch any part of the video as many times as you like

**Example:**
1. Watch to 5:00 → Progress: 5:00
2. Seek back to 2:00 → Progress: Still 5:00 (not decreased)
3. Watch to 8:00 → Progress: 8:00 (new highest)

## Resume Functionality

Never lose your place! The system automatically remembers where you left off.

### How Resume Works

1. **Automatic Detection**: When you return to a lesson, the system checks your saved progress
2. **Resume Prompt**: If you've watched more than 5 seconds, you'll see a "Resume" prompt
3. **Two Options**:
   - **Resume**: Continue from where you left off
   - **Start Over**: Begin from the beginning

### Resume Prompt

![Resume Prompt Example](../images/resume-prompt.png)

The prompt shows:
- Last watched position (e.g., "Resume at 2:30")
- Completion percentage
- Two clear buttons: "Resume" and "Start Over"

### When Resume Appears

| Watch Time | Resume Prompt? |
|------------|----------------|
| 0-5 seconds | No (video starts from beginning) |
| 5+ seconds | Yes (prompt appears) |
| Completed video | No (video starts from beginning for review) |

**Tips:**
- The 5-second threshold prevents prompts from accidental clicks
- You can always seek manually if you dismiss the prompt
- Resume works across devices (watch on desktop, continue on mobile)

## Completion & Rewards

Earn points by completing lessons!

### Completion Criteria

A lesson is considered **complete** when you reach **80% of the video duration**.

**Why 80%?**
- Accounts for end credits or recap sections
- Ensures you've watched the core content
- Prevents accidental completions from seeking

**Example:**
- 10-minute video: Complete at 8:00 (80% of 10:00)
- 30-minute video: Complete at 24:00 (80% of 30:00)

### Point Rewards

**What You Earn:**
- **Points**: Varies by lesson (typically 50-100 points)
- **Badge**: Green checkmark appears on completed lessons
- **Progress**: Course completion percentage updates

**Notification:**
When you complete a lesson, you'll see:
1. **Confetti Animation**: Celebratory visual effect
2. **Points Notification**: "Congratulations! +50 points earned"
3. **Auto-Dismiss**: Notification disappears after 5 seconds

### One-Time Rewards

- **First Completion**: Earn points when you first complete the lesson
- **Subsequent Views**: No additional points for re-watching
- **Review Freely**: Watch completed lessons as many times as you like

## Troubleshooting

### Video Won't Load

**Possible Causes:**
- Slow internet connection
- YouTube service issues
- Invalid video ID

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Internet**: Ensure you have a stable connection
2. **Refresh Page**: Press F5 or Ctrl+R to reload
3. **Try Another Browser**: Switch to Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
4. **Clear Cache**: Clear browser cache and cookies
5. **Contact Support**: If issue persists, report it using the "Report Issue" button

### Progress Not Saving

**Symptoms:**
- Progress resets when you refresh
- "Progress saved" indicator doesn't appear

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Login**: Ensure you're logged in to your account
2. **Check Network**: Verify internet connection
3. **Wait 10 Seconds**: Progress saves every 10 seconds, not instantly
4. **Check Browser Storage**: Ensure cookies/localStorage are enabled
5. **Offline Queue**: Progress may be queued if offline - it syncs when you reconnect

### Resume Not Working

**Why Resume Might Not Appear:**
- Watched less than 5 seconds (threshold not met)
- Already completed the lesson (starts from beginning)
- Progress not saved from previous session

**Solutions:**
1. **Watch Longer**: Watch at least 5 seconds before leaving
2. **Check Progress**: Verify progress saved (look for "X% Complete")
3. **Manual Seek**: Use progress bar to jump to your position

### Keyboard Shortcuts Not Working

**Common Issues:**
- Player not focused (no blue outline)
- Browser shortcuts interfering
- Input field has focus instead

**Solutions:**
1. **Focus Player**: Click on the video player or press Tab
2. **Check Focus**: Look for blue outline around player
3. **Close Dialogs**: Close any open modals or popups
4. **Reload Page**: Refresh if shortcuts stop responding

### Playback Speed Doesn't Change

**Issue:**
- Speed button clicked but video still plays at 1x

**Solutions:**
1. **Verify Selection**: Current speed button should be highlighted
2. **Reload Video**: Pause and resume playback
3. **Try Another Speed**: Click a different speed, then click desired speed again

### Points Not Awarded

**Why Points Might Not Be Awarded:**
- Didn't reach 80% completion threshold
- Already earned points for this lesson
- Server error during completion

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Completion**: Verify you're at 80%+ in the video
2. **Check History**: View your transaction history to confirm
3. **Watch to End**: Ensure you reach the 80% mark
4. **Contact Support**: If points missing after completion, report issue

### Premium Content Not Accessible

**Issue:**
- Premium badge shown but video won't play

**Solutions:**
1. **Verify Subscription**: Check your account settings for active premium subscription
2. **Refresh Page**: Sometimes subscription status needs to sync
3. **Log Out/In**: Sign out and sign back in to refresh session
4. **Contact Support**: If subscription active but access denied

## Tips for Best Experience

### Optimal Viewing

✅ **Do:**
- Use latest browser version (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
- Enable JavaScript and cookies
- Use headphones for better audio
- Watch in quiet environment for focus
- Take notes while watching
- Use slower speeds for complex topics

❌ **Avoid:**
- Watching on very slow connections (< 1 Mbps)
- Multiple tabs playing video simultaneously
- Excessive seeking (wait for buffer)
- Skipping too far ahead before watching

### Learning Tips

1. **Active Learning**: Pause to take notes at key points
2. **Review**: Use rewind to review difficult concepts
3. **Speed Up**: Increase speed for familiar review content
4. **Complete Fully**: Reach 80% to earn points and track progress
5. **Sequential**: Watch lessons in order for best understanding
6. **Practice**: Apply concepts immediately after watching
